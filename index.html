Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-20

This Privacy Policy explains how Tic-Tac-Toe (package: com.candykimy.tictactoe) handles information.

1) Overview

Tic-Tac-Toe is a mobile game. We do not require account registration and we do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number inside the app.

2) Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app uses Google AdMob to show ads.

AdMob and its partners may collect certain data automatically for ad delivery, fraud prevention, analytics, and ad personalization depending on your device settings and region. This may include:

  • Device and app identifiers
  • IP address
  • Approximate location
  • Ad interaction and performance data
  • Diagnostics and crash-related data

For details about how Google processes data, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps

3) Analytics and crash data

We may use platform-level diagnostics and ad network diagnostics to identify crashes or technical issues and improve app stability.

4) Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data processed by third-party SDK providers (such as Google AdMob) is handled according to their own privacy policies.

5) Children

The app is intended for a general audience. If local laws require child-directed treatment, ad personalization behavior depends on platform/ad network settings and compliance configurations.

6) Security

We take reasonable technical measures to keep the app and integrations secure. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this URL and become effective when published.
